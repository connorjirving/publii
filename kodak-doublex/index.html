The Peak District on 1959 Cinematic Film

Connor Irving April 26, 2020

Kodak Double-X, mostly referred to as "5222" was introduced in 1959 and was used in a cinematic sense. Steven Spielberg shot Double-X to shoot Schindler's List.

Kodak Double-X 250. Developed in D-76. Shot with Ricoh XR-2.
