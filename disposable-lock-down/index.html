Disposable Lock Down

Connor Irving May 28, 2020

Lockdown has interrupted creative thinking. A way to break the repetition of shooting with the same camera, I decided to take some images with a simple Disposable camera, one that used to be part of a Tesco truck driver's kit bag as to document any kind of collision the driver may be involved with. The film stock is still a mystery as it is only branded with "Colour" and it's speed. Research uncovers that it could be either Agfa or Ferrania.

Mystery Film. Developed in Caffenol. Shot with Disposable.
